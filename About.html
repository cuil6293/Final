Our Story

Our founding

Want to learn more about someone? Just pop by WeCU! We are a small company that's been built from the ground up by our founder Gibus Yor Daytah when he found out that there was a severe drought of data in the market. Since 1996, our founder has been working on ways to mine data ethically and efficiently by himself. He recruited a few people he knew and eventually WeCU grew into the company it is today.

We care about you!

As one of the top companies that provide data, we understand that you're scared that we might be taking your data, but we care about your privacy. On WeCU, your privacy is safe, since we know how valuable it can be, we treat it with care.
